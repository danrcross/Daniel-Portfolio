About Me

I am a novice web developer. I am currently enrolled in an online web development bootcamp through the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am motivated by the satisfaction that this work brings, both as an individual and in the role I could play in a community. Creating something useful for others gives me joy.

In my experience so far, coding involves a great deal of logical problem solving tasks. I have always loved these kinds of puzzles. I am confident that I will be able to work through any challenge that I might meet in my coding career. Already, it has been a pleasure to get some webpages up and running, no matter how simple they might be at this stage.
